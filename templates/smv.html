Perceived Percent: This is the percent that a woman will perceive a man to be. Throughout a woman's life the importance of physical traits (Height or Leanness) vs provisioning traits (Income) shift. When a woman considers a man, the perceived percent is that value that she internalizes.

Actual Percent: This is the unmitigated percent that a man is at. It is more of a true value than the obfuscated value that perception introduces.

Ultimately, only perceived SMV matters, but context is helpful.
